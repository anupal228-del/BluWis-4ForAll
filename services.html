Our Services

BluWis delivers SAP services through a structured Plan–Build–Run framework designed to reduce risk, preserve clean-core integrity, and enable long-term innovation.

01. PLAN

Strategy & Readiness

Purpose: To establish architectural clarity, governance, and organizational readiness before execution begins.

What We Do

  • Assess S/4HANA readiness across systems, data, and processes
  • Define clean-core architecture principles
  • Rationalize applications and customizations
  • Design future-state SAP landscapes
  • Establish transformation governance and controls

Business Value

Reduced rework | Lower long-term maintenance cost | Faster decision-making | Predictable delivery outcomes

02. BUILD

Implementation & Delivery

Purpose: To deliver SAP transformations with discipline, transparency, and architectural integrity.

What We Do

  • Execute S/4HANA transformations (Greenfield, Brownfield, Selective)
  • Enforce clean-core design during build
  • Integrate SAP with enterprise systems
  • Execute testing, controls, and compliance frameworks
  • Enable business adoption and change management

Business Value

Reduced delivery risk | Controlled scope and customization | Higher adoption at go-live | Stable system performance

03. RUN

Post Go-Live & Optimization

Purpose: To protect business continuity and continuously improve SAP performance.

What We Do

  • Hypercare and stabilization
  • Continuous optimization
  • Release and change governance
  • Monitoring and resilience planning
  • Performance tuning

Business Value

Faster stabilization | Lower operational disruption | Sustained ROI | Platform readiness for innovation
